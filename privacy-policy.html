Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

At Ledger Security Devices ("we", "us", "our"), the privacy of our visitors and customers is fundamental to our business. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this website (the "Site"), located at ledgerswalllet.org, how we use and protect it, and the choices you have. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Contact details — your name and email address when you email our support team or subscribe to product notifications.
  • Support inquiry details — the device model, serial number, and a description of the issue you report to our support team.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, referring URLs, browser type, screen size, approximate region, and time spent on the Site.
  • Device identifiers — IP address and a randomized visitor identifier used to distinguish unique sessions.

1.3 What we do not collect

Your Ledger hardware device generates and stores your private credentials entirely offline. We never receive, store, or have access to your device PIN, your recovery phrase, or any of the credentials registered to your accounts. Those remain solely on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to your support inquiries and warranty requests.
  • To improve the Site's content, performance, and compatibility.
  • To send you product firmware advisories and security notifications you have opted in to receive.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues affecting the Site.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Site uses a minimal set of first-party cookies and local storage to remember your preferences (such as navigation state) and to collect aggregate usage analytics. We do not use cookies to build a profile of your off-site activity. You can disable cookies in your browser; some Site features may not function as intended if you do.

4. Analytics and Advertising

We may use third-party analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. If we serve advertising through third-party platforms (for example, Google Ads), we comply with the platform's policy requirements, including providing this Privacy Policy and any necessary consent mechanisms. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers for their independent use.

5. How We Share Information

  • Service providers — we engage trusted vendors for hosting, email delivery, and analytics. They process information on our behalf and under written confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal compliance — where required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred subject to the protections of this policy.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described here, to comply with our legal obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements. Aggregate and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information.
  • Withdraw consent to processing performed on the basis of consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@ledgerswalllet.org.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. That is precisely why our hardware devices keep your most sensitive credentials offline.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Users

The Site is hosted in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the Site, you consent to that transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with an updated "Last updated" date. Material changes will be highlighted at the top of the page.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@ledgerswalllet.org
  • Support: support@ledgerswalllet.org
  • Website: ledgerswalllet.org
