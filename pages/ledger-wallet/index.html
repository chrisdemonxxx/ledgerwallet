What is Ledger Live?

Ledger Live is the official companion app for Ledger hardware security devices. It lets you set up your device, pair it with your computer or phone, manage your account credentials, and verify security actions from one place.

  • Set up and configure your Ledger device
  • Pair via Bluetooth or USB-C
  • Manage 15,000+ accounts and credentials
  • Verify transactions and authentication on-device
  • Install firmware updates
  • Manage multiple devices from one app

Getting Started

  1. Download the app for your platform above.
  2. Connect your Ledger device via USB-C or Bluetooth.
  3. Set your PIN directly on the device.
  4. Write down your 24-word recovery phrase and store it securely offline.
  5. Pair the device with Ledger Live when prompted.
  6. You're ready — start managing your credentials securely.
