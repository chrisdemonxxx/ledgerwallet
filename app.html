How to update your device

1

Back up your recovery phrase

Before any firmware change, confirm your one-time recovery phrase is safely stored offline.

2

Install the device manager

Download the official Ledger Device Manager desktop utility to check for and install firmware updates securely.

3

Connect & confirm

Plug in your device via USB-C, accept the update prompt on the device screen, and let the installation complete without disconnecting.

Security advisories

Transparent disclosure is part of our commitment. Below is a record of resolved advisories.

Advisory LSA-2026-04 — Resolved in firmware 2.4.3 / 1.8.2

An edge case in the Bluetooth pairing handshake could cause a pairing retry on certain host devices. No credential could be extracted. Fixed in the latest firmware.

Advisory LSA-2026-01 — Resolved in firmware 2.4.1 / 1.8.0

A minor timing side-channel in an internal diagnostic path was reported through our responsible-disclosure program. The path is disabled in production firmware; no exploitation was observed in the field.
